Advice for Diabetes

- Monitor blood sugar regularly.
- Eat low glycemic foods.
- Exercise regularly.
- Stay hydrated.
- Avoid sugary snacks and drinks.

Advice for High Blood Pressure

- Limit salt intake.
- Exercise regularly.
- Maintain a healthy weight.
- Practice stress relief techniques.
- Avoid smoking and alcohol.
